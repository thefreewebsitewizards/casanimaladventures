Through carefully curated internships, study abroad programs, and ethical wildlife adventures, I help others develop the skills and passion needed to make a real difference in conservation.

The Wild Patch Challenge

Join our growing community initiative to create wildlife-friendly spaces and document biodiversity in your local area.

What is the Wild Patch Challenge?

The Wild Patch Challenge is a new community project where participants create and maintain wildlife-friendly spaces while documenting the species they encounter. It's a hands-on way to contribute to local conservation efforts and connect with nature.

Create Your Patch

Design a wildlife-friendly space in your yard, balcony, or community area.

Document Wildlife

Record the species that visit your patch and share your observations.

Join the Community

Connect with other participants and share your conservation journey.
