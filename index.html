What is the Wild Patch Challenge?

The Wild Patch Challenge is a new community project where participants create and maintain wildlife-friendly spaces while documenting the species they encounter. It's a hands-on way to contribute to local conservation efforts and connect with nature.

Ethical Travel Guide

Learn how to travel responsibly and make a positive impact on the places and wildlife you visit.

Choose Eco-Friendly Accommodations

Look for certified sustainable lodges and hotels that prioritize environmental conservation and support local communities.

  • Green certifications
  • Local ownership
  • Renewable energy use

Respect Wildlife

Maintain safe distances, never feed wild animals, and choose ethical wildlife experiences that prioritize animal welfare.

  • Observe from distance
  • No touching or feeding
  • Support sanctuaries

Minimize Your Footprint

Reduce waste, conserve water and energy, and choose sustainable transportation options whenever possible.

  • Reusable water bottles
  • Public transportation
  • Pack light

Support Local Communities

Buy from local artisans, eat at family-owned restaurants, and hire local guides to ensure your money benefits the community.

  • Local guides
  • Community markets
  • Cultural respect

Document Responsibly

Share your experiences to inspire others while being mindful of sensitive locations and wildlife behavior.

  • No flash photography
  • Respect privacy
  • Educational content

Give Back

Participate in conservation projects, volunteer with local organizations, or contribute to wildlife protection efforts.

  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Conservation donations
  • Citizen science
